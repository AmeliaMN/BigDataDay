Generalized pairs plot

Do you know the scatterplot matrix or pairs plot? Generally, it looks like this:

plot(mtcars)

That's a lot to look at, so lets take a subset:

library(dplyr)
select(mtcars, mpg, cyl, disp) %>% plot()
